# Tribunal of Human Expansion (T.H.E.)

The **Tribunal of Human Expansion (T.H.E.)** is a team of Computer Science Master’s students at **Trinity College Dublin** building a globally distributed traffic authorization system.

Our project explores how large-scale distributed systems manage coordination, consistency, and failure across regions Our system enforces a simple rule:

> Every road journey must be authorized before departure.

## What We’re Building

**THE Traffic Authority** — a global system that:

* Accepts journey booking requests
* Detects conflicts and capacity constraints
* Approves, delays, or rejects travel permits
* Coordinates across multiple regions
* Demonstrates resilience under simulated failures

## Focus Areas

* Multi-region architecture
* Consistency vs availability tradeoffs
* Fault tolerance & recovery
* Observability & system health
* Failure injection and degraded modes

We’re interested in how distributed systems behave when things go wrong.
